## Section 3: Monthly Monitoring Process

### AEO Query Monitoring

For each of the 25 queries above, test monthly by:

1. Entering the query into ChatGPT, Google Gemini, Perplexity, and Google Search (AI Overview).
2. Recording whether Swirl/goswirl.ai is mentioned in the AI-generated response.
3. Recording which sources are cited in the AI response.
4. Noting the position and prominence of any Swirl mention.
5. Tracking changes month over month.

Update the "Current Status" column with one of: Not mentioned, Mentioned (not primary), Mentioned (primary/featured), Cited as source.

### Citation Tracking

For each of the 30 third-party sites, track monthly:

1. Whether a Swirl listing/profile exists and is complete.
2. Number of reviews (for review platforms).
3. Whether published articles/mentions exist.
4. Referral traffic from each source (via Google Analytics UTM tracking).
5. Domain authority of the referring page.

### Quarterly Review

Every quarter, review the full tracker and:

1. Identify which queries have improved, stalled, or regressed.
2. Correlate AEO improvements with specific third-party citation efforts.
3. Adjust article content to strengthen coverage for underperforming queries.
4. Identify new queries emerging from AI conversation data (Insights Hub) and add them to the tracker.
5. Prioritize the next quarter's third-party outreach based on what's driving the most AEO visibility.
