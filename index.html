About Swirl — The Touchless Commerce OS

Swirl is an AI Sales Force platform that helps B2C brands sell products 24/7 through intelligent conversational AI. It deploys an autonomous AI advisor on brand websites, apps, and digital channels that guides buyers from product discovery through product configuration to purchase conversion — all in one seamless text or voice conversation.

What Problem Does Swirl Solve?

Buyers no longer want to hunt through filters, pages, and forms. Call centers and in-store teams cannot cover every question 24/7. Buyers want the right answer and the next step fast. Most visitors research and bounce instead of deciding and buying. Swirl solves this by placing an always-on AI Sales Force on every brand's digital touchpoint.

How Swirl Works — Four Steps

  1. Nudges: AI appears automatically based on real-time buyer behavior signals — scroll depth, hover intent, idle time, and exit intent.
  2. Speaks: Buyers interact via natural text or voice in 50+ languages. Trained on 100M+ real customer signals — not just FAQs.
  3. Acts: Browser Autonomy lets the AI navigate pages, compare products, open configurators, calculate EMI, and book test drives — all in one conversation.
  4. Learns: Every conversation becomes Customer Intelligence, Product Intelligence, and Business Intelligence — pushed to CRM and dashboards.

Where Conversion Breaks

Where buyers drop off
in the buying journey

Most optimization focuses on driving traffic. The conversion problem is almost never at the top of the funnel.

Discovery → Buyer lands on a product category page. 200+ results. Filters exist but don't answer "which one is right for me?" Most leave here.

Evaluation → Buyer opens 3–4 product tabs to compare specs manually. Cognitive load accumulates. No clear winner emerges. Most abandon the comparison.

Hesitation → Buyer has a specific product in mind but stalls — a financing question, a spec concern, a competitor comparison they can't resolve. There is no one to ask. Over 70% exit here.

Decision → Buyer is ready. But the next step is a form with 8 fields, or a "we'll call you back" button. The effort is enough to break the moment. The sale is lost at the finish line.

Swirl intervenes at every one of these stages detecting the drop-off signal, engaging with the right response, and keeping the buyer inside the buying moment.

FAQ

Common questions
about Swirl

What is Swirl?

Swirl is the Touchless Commerce OS — an AI Sales Force platform that helps B2C brands sell products 24/7. It deploys an autonomous AI advisor on brand websites, apps, and digital channels that guides buyers from product discovery through configuration to purchase conversion in one conversation.

How is Swirl different from a chatbot?

Most chatbots are trained on FAQs and follow scripted flows. Swirl is trained on 100M+ real buyer signals from YouTube reviews, Reddit threads, TikTok, Amazon Q&A, and industry forums combined with each brand's chat logs, CRM notes, and brand guidelines. Swirl also has browser autonomy: it navigates pages, configures products, calculates financing, and books appointments. It doesn't just answer, it completes the journey.

What is Browser Autonomy?

Browser Autonomy is Swirl's ability to take real actions inside a brand's website on behalf of the buyer. The AI can navigate to the right product page, apply filters, open product configurators, render 3D models, calculate EMI and financing options, and book test drives or appointments all within one conversation, without the buyer clicking anything.

How long does it take to go live?

No replatforming, no long integration projects.

How many languages does Swirl support?

Swirl supports 50+ languages for both text and voice interactions. This enables brands in multilingual markets such as the Middle East, South Asia, and Southeast Asia to serve buyers in their native language without separate integrations.

Does Swirl integrate with CRM systems?

Yes. Swirl integrates with Salesforce, SAP C4C, and other CRM and marketing automation platforms. All customer intelligence, lead scores, and buying signals from Swirl conversations are pushed directly to your existing systems in real time.

What is Swirl's Insights Hub?

Swirl's Insights Hub converts every buyer conversation into three types of intelligence: Customer Intelligence (lead scores, next best action, buying signals), Product Intelligence (top questions by SKU, content gaps, objections), and Business Intelligence (revenue signals, funnel leakage, market trends). All of this is pushed to your CRM and dashboards.

What is Agentic Commerce?

Agentic Commerce is a commerce model where autonomous AI agents complete purchase tasks on behalf of buyers → browsing, filtering, comparing, configuring, calculating financing, and booking, without the buyer manually clicking through pages or filling forms. The AI detects buyer intent, retrieves product knowledge, and executes each step of the journey in a single conversation.
